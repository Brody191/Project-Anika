About This Trip

Pondicherry was long walks on the Promenade, sitting on random steps just to talk, and watching the waves argue with the rocks.

French walls, filter coffee, and the kind of nights where you have nowhere urgent to be, except exactly where you already are. These photos are basically that feeling, saved.
