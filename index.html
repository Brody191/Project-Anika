How Did We Meet?

One day, three autumns

(Yī rì sān qiū)

A chinese idiom that is used when you miss someone so much, one day feels like long as three years.

An Extremely Long Time Ago, there existed a separate version of me, and a separate version of her.

  • Then we decided to Meet. It all Started on July 15th 2024. When.
  • She joined us, I was already done in life and cooked from every direction possible, I was so deprived of intellectual simulation and conversations with someone who had the ability to use their frontal cortex before moving their lips, That is when she came into my life.

And just like that, everything changed.
